Nice to Meet YOU!

Swastik Metal have Expertise in Brass Manufacturing and Global Scrap Importing

Since 1997, Swastik Metal has been a cornerstone in the manufacturing industry, delivering a wide range of high-quality brass products. Our extensive product line is utilized across various sectors, including the electric industry, automation industry, and medical industry, ensuring superior performance and reliability. With over four decades of experience, we have established a reputation for excellence and innovation, exporting our products worldwide.

In addition to manufacturing, Swastik Metal boasts over 10-12 years of expertise in brass scrap processing. We are a leading importer of Brass Scrap (Honey Brass), sourcing materials from around the globe to maintain our commitment to sustainability and quality. Our global network ensures we receive the finest scrap materials, which are then meticulously processed to create top-tier brass products.

At Swastik Metal, our dedication to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction drives us to continually improve our processes and expand our reach. Join us in our journey as we continue to set the standard in the brass manufacturing industry.
