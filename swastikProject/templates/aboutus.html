Swastik Metal have Expertise in Brass Manufacturing and Global Scrap Importing

Established in the heart of Jamnagar—the "Brass City of India"—Swastik Metal is a trusted name in precision brass manufacturing. We specialize in high-quality brass components, including inserts, rods, medical parts, ball valves, nut-bolts, electric components, and stamping parts, serving industries like automotive, agriculture, construction, and medical equipment. Our commitment to quality and innovation ensures precision and reliability in every product.

Beyond manufacturing, Swastik Metal is a key player in the brass scrap industry, dealing in high-quality materials like Honey Scrap and Vilayti Scrap. We focus on sustainable solutions, connecting local and global markets with eco-friendly and reliable services.

Our mission is to blend craftsmanship with innovation while fostering lasting client relationships. Whether through premium components or sustainable scrap solutions, we prioritize quality, efficiency, and customer satisfaction, striving to set new standards in the brass industry.
